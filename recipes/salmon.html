Pan-seared Salmon is an easy, quick, and delicious recipe that needs only a few ingredients and only takes about 30 min to make

Ingredients

  • Salt and Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Parsely
  • Butter
  • Vegtable Oil

Instructions

  1. Wash salmon with cold water and dry with paper towels
  2. Season with all seasonings listed above and let sit for about 10 min
  3. Heat pan to medium high
  4. Place vegtable oil and spread around pan, add 1/4 table spoon of butter
  5. Place salmon on pan scales down, let cook for about 3 min or until more than half of fish is white
  6. Flip lift salmon and cook other side for 2 min or slight char is visible
  7. Remove salmon from pan and let site for about 2 min
  8. Add any sides you desire
