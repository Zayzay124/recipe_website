Chicken Sandwich

Home

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs or breast
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Italian
  • Pickle Slices
  • Sauce of choice
  • Seasame Buns
  • Butter
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with desired seasonings
  2. Heat pan to medium-low
  3. Place 1/2 tablespoon of butter into pan and let melt
  4. Place buns into pan seed side up
  5. Remove once golden brown
  6. In same pan add tablespoon of olive oil and spread around pan(more butter can be added as well if desired)
  7. Place chicken in pan and let both sides cook for about 3 minutes
  8. Assemble sandwich
